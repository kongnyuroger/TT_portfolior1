# TikToker Portfolio Website Walkthrough

I have completed the development of the modern one-page TikToker portfolio website. The project uses Next.js 15, Tailwind CSS v4, and Framer Motion to create a high-converting, premium experience.

## Key Accomplishments

### 1. Modern Design & Branding
- **Electric Blue Theme**: Implemented a vibrant Electric Blue highlight color consistently across CTAs, accents, and glows.
- **Dark Aesthetic**: Used a custom dark/neutral palette with glassmorphism for a high-end personal brand feel.
- **Premium Typography**: Integrated Geist Sans for a clean, professional look.

### 2. Interactive Components
- **Sticky Glass Navbar**: Smooth scrolling and mobile-responsive menu.
- **Animated Social Proof**: Stats counters for views, followers, and engagement.
- **Video Grid**: Modern card layout for TikTok content with high-energy hover effects.
- **Floating CTA**: Persistent "Work With Me" button to maximize conversions.

### 3. Comprehensive Sections
- **Hero**: High-impact headline with catchphrases and dual CTAs.
- **Collaboration**: Tailored offerings for Brands (UGC, Ads) and Creators.
- **Mentorship**: Highlighted section for 1-on-1 coaching services.
- **Testimonials**: Social proof from past clients and students.
- **Contact**: Clean lead generation form with social integration.
